# Scoring

## Summary Scores

| Dimension | Score | Justification |
|---|---:|---|
| Coverage | 9/10 | The repository covers nearly the full consultancy lifecycle: acquisition, onboarding, strategy, planning, platform design, execution playbooks, reporting, reviews, and retention. The main reason it is not a 10 is that some delivery layers are intentionally out of scope, especially ad-ops management, design/video production, and web implementation. |
| Strategy Depth | 8/10 | Strategy is one of the system's strongest areas. Multiple frameworks are available and mapped across channel, funnel, content, and business objectives. It is not a 9 or 10 because some skills still rely on market defaults rather than a formal decision layer for cross-market adaptation. |
| Execution Strength | 7/10 | The playbook layer is highly actionable and suitable for agency operations, but the repository stops at guidance and operating design. It does not execute media buying, creative testing, production, or implementation tasks that many agencies need to control directly. |
| AI Integration | 9/10 | AI is deeply and intelligently integrated across readiness, prompting, automation, quality control, ethics, IP, bias, and humanisation. This is well beyond superficial prompt-pack usage. The remaining gap is stronger linkage between AI workflows and market-specific adaptation logic. |
| Platform Expertise | 8/10 | Platform coverage is broad and practically useful. The skills show understanding of platform roles, content mechanics, and channel-specific strategy. The gap is that global platform usage differences and regional platform substitutions are not handled systematically. |
| Analytics & ROI | 8/10 | Reporting, ROI, business-case, revenue planning, UTM discipline, testing, and dashboard thinking are all present. This is a major strength. The score stops at 8 because cross-channel attribution maturity and country-specific privacy/tracking adaptations are not consistently embedded across the whole suite. |
| Client-Facing Quality | 8/10 | Proposals, decks, reporting structures, and governance outputs are professional and persuasive. The system is clearly capable of producing premium-looking consulting deliverables. It is not a 9 or 10 because some outputs still need stronger board-level adaptation for non-EA markets and enterprise contexts. |
| System Integration | 8/10 | The repository behaves like a coherent operating system rather than a loose pile of prompts. Naming, sequencing, and lifecycle logic are strong. The main weakness is that the context-adaptation layer is not yet centralised, so global execution requires manual stitching. |
| Real-World Usability | 7/10 | A competent consultant can absolutely use this to run a real practice, especially a strategy-led boutique agency. The score is held back because execution dependencies outside the repo are significant, and global adaptation currently depends too much on operator experience. |
| Context Adaptability | 5/10 | This is the critical weak point. The system is East Africa-first and only partially global. Some legal and language awareness exists, but there is no system-wide mechanism for changing assumptions by country, culture, regulation, buying behaviour, or platform usage. |
| Output Quality Potential | 8/10 | With a strong operator, the repository can produce premium consulting outputs. The ceiling is high because the strategic and reporting scaffolding is good. The limit is that output quality still depends on manual localisation for many non-EA contexts. |

## Overall Score

**Overall score:** 7.7/10

## Interpretation

This score represents a system that is commercially serious, strategically capable, and genuinely useful in real consultancy work. It already exceeds the quality bar of most marketing prompt libraries because it contains operating logic, commercial documents, measurement frameworks, and AI governance.

The reason it does not score higher is not a lack of skill quantity. It is the absence of a formal global adaptation layer. Until that exists, the system is best described as a high-quality East Africa-first, multi-market consultancy engine rather than a world-class global consultancy engine.
