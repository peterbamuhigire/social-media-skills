# Phase 04 - Discovery And Onboarding Retrofit

## Objective

Refit the repository's discovery and intake layer so every engagement collects the information required for truly market-aware, strategically rigorous downstream work.

## Why This Phase Is Critical

A context engine only works if the consultancy captures the right inputs. Right now the repo has strong onboarding skills, but they do not yet force the operator to gather enough market, regulatory, cultural, procurement, and business-model data for world-class consultancy work.

## Core Outputs

1. Retrofit `00-client-intake`, `01-client-brief`, `02-platform-audit`, `03-audience-personas`, and `04-brand-voice-intake`.
2. Add enterprise and regulated-sector discovery questions.
3. Add market-readiness and internal stakeholder mapping fields.
4. Add stronger competitor and category maturity assessment.

## Repository Work

### 1. Upgrade intake forms

Add required fields for:

- country or countries in scope,
- priority customer segments by market,
- internal approval chain,
- risk sensitivity,
- language variants,
- category maturity,
- procurement constraints,
- legal/compliance concern areas,
- existing martech stack,
- conversion pathway by market.

### 2. Upgrade platform audit logic

The platform audit should examine:

- current platform relevance by market,
- mismatch between platform effort and market behaviour,
- localisation quality,
- trust-signal gaps,
- attribution readiness,
- business-goal alignment.

### 3. Upgrade audience persona logic

Personas should move beyond demographics into:

- functional pains,
- trust barriers,
- cultural expectations,
- preferred proof,
- preferred channel journey,
- purchase objections,
- language and tone norms,
- decision-making style.

### 4. Upgrade brand voice intake

Voice capture should include:

- what the brand sounds like across markets,
- where adaptation is allowed,
- what cannot change,
- taboo wording,
- authority level,
- humour range,
- evidence style,
- executive versus consumer tone variants.

## Skills And Files Most Affected

- `00-client-intake`
- `01-client-brief`
- `02-platform-audit`
- `03-audience-personas`
- `04-brand-voice-intake`
- `brand-voice-ai-training`

## Completion Criteria

- Downstream strategy skills receive materially better inputs.
- The repository can distinguish a local SME brief from a multi-market, regulated, or enterprise brief at the onboarding stage.
- Market assumptions are captured before strategy generation begins.
- Persona and brand voice outputs become more actionable for platform, content, and conversion planning.

## Risks To Control

- Making discovery forms so long that they become unusable.
- Capturing more information without improving decision quality.
- Asking for data that clients cannot realistically provide without offering alternatives.

## Reading Material To Buy For This Stage

- *Buyer Personas* by Adele Revella  
  Use it to strengthen persona depth around buying decisions, objections, and triggering events.
- *Building a StoryBrand 2.0* by Donald Miller  
  Use it to clarify customer problem framing, offer articulation, and message hierarchy.
- *Obviously Awesome* by April Dunford  
  Use it to improve positioning questions and category framing during intake and brief development.
