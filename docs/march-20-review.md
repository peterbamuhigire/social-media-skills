**Verdict:** Ready to win and retain clients at a world-class level.

### 2. Strategy and Planning — EXCELLENT (24/24)

The strategy layer is comprehensive: social, digital, email, influencer, and campaign strategies each have dedicated skills. Five advanced strategic frameworks go beyond basics — organic/paid hybrid, customer value journey, channel architecture, PDCA execution loop, and creator monetisation. Content planning has full coverage from pillars to calendar to campaign brief.

**Verdict:** Can deliver board-level strategies that stand alongside Big Four consulting outputs.

### 3. Platform Execution — STRONG (11 platforms + 36 playbooks)

All major EA platforms are covered with dedicated strategy skills. The playbook library is exceptional: 36 execution guides covering community management, crisis communications, governance, paid social, social selling, DM sales funnels, employee advocacy, UGC, viral content, and more. The `playbook-geo-newsjacking` skill, added in this batch, completes real-time content capability.

**Gap:** No skill for emerging platforms (Threads, BeReal, Pinterest). Low priority for EA market.

**Verdict:** Complete for EA market. Outperforms most EA agencies by significant margin.

### 4. Analytics and Measurement — EXCELLENT (25 skills)

Analytics coverage is unusually deep for a content-focused agency. The suite covers competitor analysis, content audits, ROI frameworks, UTM tracking, A/B testing, algorithm guides, budget planning, cohort analysis, dashboard design, lead scoring, revenue planning, and privacy-compliant analytics setup. The `meta-social-proof-system` (Bly taxonomy) adds systematic social proof to the mix.

**Verdict:** Can compete with specialist analytics firms on measurement rigour.

### 5. Training and Enablement — STRONG (6 skills)

Training covers client teams, DIY content creators, social media fundamentals, smartphone video, AI prompt writing, and AI foundations. The AI training skills have been significantly upgraded with Co-Pilot vs Co-Thinker frameworks and Three-Wave AI evolution models.

**Gap:** No sales team social selling training guide (distinct from the `playbook-social-selling` operational guide).

**Verdict:** Strong. One gap in sales team enablement.

### 6. Business Development Tools — COMPLETE (Fihn model)

The three Fihn-model skills (`biz-dev-video-outreach`, `biz-dev-beyond-agency-offer`, `biz-dev-reactivation-campaign`) together with `biz-dev-practitioner-positioning` give the agency a complete new-business system: personal positioning → warm outreach → risk-free offer → dormant list reactivation.
