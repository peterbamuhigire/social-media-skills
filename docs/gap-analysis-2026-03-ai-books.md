## Core Principle from All Books

Every book converges on one point: **AI must be used but must not sound like AI.** The quality gap between raw AI output and human-quality marketing content is the single most important problem to solve. The books identify:

- AI output lacks depth, nuance, emotional intelligence, and cultural specificity
- Generic AI content is increasingly invisible in an AI-saturated market (Schaefer: "pandemic of dull")
- The most valuable role for the consultant is quality control, brand voice enforcement, and cultural localisation — none of which AI can do without a human
- AI democratises production; human creativity, community trust, and cultural knowledge become the competitive moat

**For this skill suite:** Every strategy and plan skill should include a specific section on how AI is used within that deliverable AND how quality is controlled to ensure the output sounds human.

## Existing Skills Requiring AI Updates

### `playbook-ai-content-workflow` — Major update needed
- Add the Alpha-Beta-Gamma-Delta-Epsilon prompt structure (Upadhyay)
- Add the 7 copywriting frameworks (PAS, AIDA, BAB, FAB, SSS, PPPP, AFOREST)
- Add a mandatory human review gate with a 5-point quality checklist
- Add brand voice calibration as a named step before generating any content
- Add the cultural localisation step specific to Uganda/EA markets
- Add a "What AI Cannot Do" section (Schaefer): read local cultural tensions, produce genuinely novel ideas, create collective effervescence, replicate unscripted employee voice
- Add the 4-stage content maturity model (Upadhyay): basic → aligned → multichannel → automated
- Add the "Proof of Human" concept — when and how to signal authentic human authorship
- Reference new skills: `ai-content-humaniser`, `brand-voice-ai-training`, `prompt-engineering-library`

### `policy-ai-content-ethics` — Update needed
- Add the 5 ethical AI principles (Ltifi, Johnsen): transparency, fairness, nonmaleficence, accountability, privacy
- Add filter bubble risk — AI personalisation creating echo chambers (Johnsen)
- Add virtual/AI influencer disclosure requirements (Ltifi: Lil Miquela precedent)
- Add data consent requirements — explicit consent before feeding customer data to AI tools
- Add the "Proof of Human" labelling concept (Schaefer)
- Add leakage risk — Samsung/ChatGPT incident (Venkatesan)
- Add impersonation risk — deepfakes of brand spokespeople (Venkatesan)
- Add ownership risk — AI-generated content copyright uncertainty (Venkatesan)
- Reference Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019 explicitly
- Add jailbreak awareness clause (Venkatesan)

### All `05-` through `09-` strategy skills
Add a standard "AI in This Strategy" section covering:
- Which tasks in this deliverable to use AI for
- Which tasks must remain human-led
- The specific prompt approach for this skill type
- Quality control steps before client delivery

### `11-content-calendar`, `10-content-pillars`
- Add e.l.f. Beauty "speed of culture" principle — leave room for rapid cultural signal response
- Add "Talk Trigger" as a content category designed for person-to-person sharing (Schaefer)
